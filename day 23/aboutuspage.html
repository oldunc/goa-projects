yeah about us

Dave's Hot Chicken is a popular fast-casual chain serving Nashville-style hot chicken tenders and sliders, famous for its seven heat levels from "No Spice" to " Reaper " (requiring a waiver) and sides like fries, mac & cheese, and kale slaw, originating from a 2017 Hollywood parking lot pop-up and rapidly expanding globally with celebrity investors and private equity backing from Roark Capital .
